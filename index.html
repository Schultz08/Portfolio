About Me

I am a dedicated and self motivated individual. I joined the Army after graduating High School and spent seven glorious years in the Army as an infantryman. After my honorable discharge from the Army I worked for various security companies for five years. I however I did not feel any fulfillment in the security field. With some advice from a friend I decided to attend Eleven Fifty Academy and thus far I have thoroughly enjoyed working with code. When I encounter an error or problem with code I thoroughly enjoy the process of figuring out the issue and when I finally fix the problem the sense of fulfillment from accomplishing the task reminds me of when I was back in the Army. This is why I want to be a Developer.
